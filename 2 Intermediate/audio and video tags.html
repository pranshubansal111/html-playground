HTML audio and video Tags

The <audio> and <video> tags allow us to embed audio and video content directly into our webpage. These tags provide built-in controls for users to play, pause, and adjust the volume of the media.

Audio Tag

Click the play button to listen to the audio. The controls attribute adds basic playback controls like play, pause, and volume adjustment.

Video Tag

Click the play button to watch the video. Like the <audio> tag, the <video> tag also has the controls attribute to provide playback controls such as play, pause, volume, and full-screen toggle.

Important Attributes of audio and video Tags:

  • controls: Provides built-in playback controls for the user (play, pause, volume control, etc.).
  • autoplay: Automatically starts playing the media when the page loads.
  • loop: Loops the media once it finishes playing.
  • muted: Mutes the audio or video.
  • width: Specifies the width of the embedded video.
  • height: Specifies the height of the embedded video.
